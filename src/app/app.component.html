Maven in Behavior

Maven in Behavior is a BCBA-founded and owned company providing high quality Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) therapy. Maven in Behavior is center-based and we work with children and adolescents diagnosed with Autism as well as individuals exhibiting maladaptive behaviors and social deficits. At Maven in Behavior, we strive to provide high quality therapy by using evidence-based approaches and interventions.

Maven in Behavior offers a variety of services at our therapy center. We offer 1:1 ABA therapy, parent training and consultations, school consultations, potty training, as well as treating picky eating and food refusal. Home and school visits may be available, upon request.

Maven in Behavior works with several insurance companies, please contact us to verify if we are in network with your plan.

To schedule a free 30 minute phone consultation, please call (305) 586-2953 or email Maven In Behavior Inc.

Services

ABA Therapy

Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) is a scientific approach to understanding behavior. Maven in Behavior’s individualized therapy programs incorporate verbal behavior and natural environment teaching to target skills such as such as communication, daily living skills, social skills, and work on decreasing problem behaviors.

The Launchpad

The Launchpad is a specialized school readiness program founded by Maven in Behavior. The Launchpad is a comprehensive ABA based school readiness program for learners ages 3-5. This program runs through the school year and also offers a summer intensive program.

Parent Training and Consulations

Trainings are always provided alongside with your child’s individualized intervention plan. Maven in Behavior provides trainings and consultations aimed to help parents better understand their child’s programming and successfully generalize acquired skills to their child’s day to day life.

School Consulations

School consultations include teacher and staff trainings as it relates to Applied Behavior Analysis. Maven in Behavior also offers classroom behavior supports to help improve classroom management strategies.

Potty Training

Potty training is a necessary life skill and critical for independent living. Maven in Behavior uses an ABA based and individualized protocol to teach the skills necessary to successfully use a bathroom independently.

Picky Eating & Food Refusal

Picky eating and food refusal are commonly seen amongst children today. Maven in Behavior uses a behavioral approach to target individualized goals such as decreasing food refusal behaviors and increasing variety in diet. To intervene successfully, it is important that all medical causes for food refusal or evaluated prior to treatment.

Team

Alana

Usategui Guilarte, M.S.,BCBA, Owner and Founder

Owner & Founder

Alana is a Board Certified Behavior Analyst and the founder and owner of Maven in Behavior Inc. For the last 15 years Alana has been working with children on the Autism Spectrum. In 2015, Alana received her Masters Degree in Applied Behavior Analysis from Nova South Eastern University. In 2017 Alana became a Board Certified Behavior Analyst and opened the doors of Maven in Behavior in December 2021.

Prior to her work as a BCBA, Alana was a special education teacher in New York City, as well as in Miami. Alana’s interest in this field began when her younger twin brothers were diagnosed with Autism and she saw first-hand the success of a well implemented ABA program. Alana also received a double bachelors degree in Psychology and Special Education from New York University.

Alana’s personal background as well as her experience in a variety of special education settings gives her a unique approach in not only how she personally tailors programs for her clients, but as well as how she relates to the staff she works with and the families she serves.
